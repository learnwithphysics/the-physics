📖 Introduction

Heat is a form of energy that flows from a hotter object to a colder object. It plays an important role in cooking, weather, industries and daily life.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand Heat.
  • Understand Temperature.
  • Learn methods of heat transfer.
  • Perform simple virtual experiments.

🔥 Heat

Heat is energy transferred because of a temperature difference.

Examples

  • ☀ Sun warming the Earth.
  • 🍳 Cooking food.
  • ☕ Hot tea cooling down.

🌡 Temperature

Temperature tells us how hot or cold an object is. A thermometer is commonly used to measure temperature.

♨ Heat Transfer

Heat can move in different ways.

  • 🔥 Conduction
  • 🌬 Convection
  • ☀ Radiation

🔬 Practical Activity

Take one metal spoon and one wooden spoon. Place both in warm water for a few minutes. Carefully touch the handles and compare how they feel.

Observation

The metal spoon becomes warm more quickly than the wooden spoon because metals transfer heat more effectively.

🌍 Heat in Daily Life

  • 🍳 Cooking food
  • 🔥 Ironing clothes
  • ☀ Solar cooker
  • 🧤 Woollen clothes reduce heat loss
  • 🥶 Refrigerators help keep food cool

📚 Improve Your Learning

1. What is Heat?

Heat is a form of energy that flows from a hotter object to a colder object.

2. What is Temperature?

Temperature tells us how hot or cold an object is.

3. Name the instrument used to measure temperature.

Thermometer.

4. Name the three methods of heat transfer.

Conduction, Convection and Radiation.

5. What is Conduction?

Conduction is the transfer of heat through direct contact.

6. What is Convection?

Convection is the transfer of heat through moving liquids or gases.

7. What is Radiation?

Radiation is the transfer of heat without any material medium.

8. Give one example of conduction.

A metal spoon becoming hot in a cup of tea.

9. Give one example of radiation.

Heat from the Sun reaching the Earth.

10. Why are woollen clothes used in winter?

They reduce heat loss from our body and help keep us warm.

📖 Lesson Summary

  • ✅ Heat is a form of energy.
  • ✅ Heat flows from a hotter object to a colder object.
  • ✅ Temperature tells how hot or cold an object is.
  • ✅ Heat transfer occurs by conduction, convection and radiation.
  • ✅ Heat is important in everyday life.
