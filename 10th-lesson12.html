📖 Introduction

The environment includes all living and non-living things around us. It provides air, water, food, shelter and natural resources required for life. Protecting the environment is essential for the survival of all living organisms.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand Environment.
  • Learn Ecosystem.
  • Know Food Chains.
  • Study Food Webs.
  • Understand Environmental Protection.

🌍 Environment

Environment consists of two main components.

🌱 Ecosystem

An ecosystem is the interaction between living organisms and their physical environment.

Main Parts

  • 🌿 Producers
  • 🐄 Consumers
  • 🍄 Decomposers

🌾 Food Chain

A food chain is the sequence in which one organism is eaten by another. Energy flows from producers to consumers.

Example

🌱 Grass → 🦗 Grasshopper → 🐸 Frog → 🐍 Snake → 🦅 Eagle

🕸 Food Web

A food web is a network of interconnected food chains. It provides stability to an ecosystem.

  • One organism can have more than one food source.
  • Many food chains are connected together.
  • Food webs maintain ecological balance.

🌍 Ozone Layer

The ozone layer is a protective layer present in the stratosphere. It absorbs most of the harmful ultraviolet (UV) rays coming from the Sun and protects living organisms.

Importance of Ozone Layer

  • 🛡 Protects living organisms from harmful UV rays.
  • 🌱 Helps plants grow safely.
  • 👨‍⚕ Reduces skin cancer and eye diseases.
  • 🌎 Maintains ecological balance.

⚠ Ozone Depletion

The thinning of the ozone layer is called ozone depletion. It mainly occurs due to Chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs) released from refrigerators, air conditioners and aerosol sprays.

Effects
  • ☀ More UV radiation reaches Earth.
  • 👨‍⚕ Skin cancer increases.
  • 👁 Eye cataracts increase.
  • 🌾 Damage to crops.
  • 🐟 Harm to aquatic organisms.

🌡 Greenhouse Effect

The greenhouse effect is the warming of the Earth due to greenhouse gases trapping heat in the atmosphere.

Greenhouse Gases

Gas Formula
Carbon Dioxide CO₂
Methane CH₄
Water Vapour H₂O
Nitrous Oxide N₂O

🔥 Global Warming

Global warming is the increase in Earth's average temperature due to excessive greenhouse gases.

Effects

  • 🌡 Increase in global temperature.
  • 🧊 Melting of glaciers.
  • 🌊 Rise in sea level.
  • 🌪 Extreme weather conditions.
  • 🌳 Loss of biodiversity.
