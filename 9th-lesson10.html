📖 Introduction

In everyday life we push, pull, lift and move objects. In Physics, work is done only when a force causes an object to move. Energy is the ability to do work.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand Work.
  • Understand Energy.
  • Learn Kinetic Energy.
  • Learn Potential Energy.
  • Perform Virtual Activities.

💪 Work

Work is done when a force acts on an object and the object moves in the direction of the force.

Examples

  • 📦 Pushing a box.
  • 🎒 Lifting a school bag.
  • 🚲 Riding a bicycle.

⚡ Energy

Energy is the ability to do work. Energy exists in many forms such as mechanical, heat, light and electrical energy.

🏃 Kinetic Energy

Kinetic energy is the energy possessed by an object because of its motion.

🪨 Potential Energy

Potential energy is the energy stored in an object because of its position.

🔬 Practical Activity

1. What is Work?

Work is done when a force causes an object to move.

2. What is Energy?

Energy is the ability to do work.

3. What is Kinetic Energy?

Kinetic energy is the energy possessed by a moving object.

4. What is Potential Energy?

Potential energy is stored energy due to position.

5. Give one example of Work.

Pushing a box or lifting a school bag.

6. Give one example of Kinetic Energy.

A moving bicycle or a rolling football.

7. Give one example of Potential Energy.

Water stored in a dam or a book kept on a shelf.

8. Can energy change from one form to another?

Yes. Energy can be transformed from one form into another.

9. What happens when a lifted object falls?

Potential energy changes into kinetic energy.

10. Name two forms of Energy.

Kinetic Energy and Potential Energy.
